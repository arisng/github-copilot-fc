# Why Ralph Uses Alias First Iterating Terminology

`ITERATING` is safe to adopt in narrative and user-facing workflow text before the repository performs a coordinated contract migration of normative enums.

## Safe Boundary

- Narrative descriptions, plans, and explanatory workflow text can adopt `ITERATING` immediately.
- Normative state and mode names such as `REPLANNING` and `UPDATE` remain authoritative until every dependent contract surface moves together.

## Design Outcome

Alias-first migration improves user-facing clarity without breaking protocol consumers or cross-role coordination tokens.

## Operational Implication

Treat full enum renames as a contract migration, not as a wording cleanup.
